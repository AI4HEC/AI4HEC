About AI4HEC

AI4HEC (AI for Human-Elephant Coexistence) is a pioneering research initiative at the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. Our mission is to develop innovative, technology-driven solutions to mitigate human-elephant conflictâ€”one of the most pressing conservation challenges in our region.

Through cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, embedded systems, and robotics, we create practical tools that protect both human communities and elephant populations. Our interdisciplinary approach combines machine learning, real-time detection systems, and smart hardware to enable peaceful coexistence.

Join us in our journey to harness technology for wildlife conservation and community safety.
